    Inverse Euler Formulas

    When solved separately for the sine and cosine terms, Euler's Formula can be transformed into the Inverse Euler Formulas. These formulas allow sine and cosine fuctions to be written as a sum of of complex exponentials. This can be usefule for decomposing things like a product of a sine and cosine function into a sum of sinusoids.
